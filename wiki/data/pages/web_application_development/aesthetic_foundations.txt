===== Brand & Theme =====

Then ask what is the website's brand or theme? What is the context of the application? Who are the target audience? What do people expect of similar websites or similar contexts? What is the message you are trying to communicate? Who do you represent? What is the point of the application and does that have a theme?

Consider MailChimp (emailing with a chimp?), Basecamp..
